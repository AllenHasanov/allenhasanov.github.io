MISSION
01
    We work to improve the well-being of our customers by reducing their costs for the purchase of high-quality everyday goods. We save people money so that they can live better. We strive to provide our customers with the best service and improve it daily. We want to be a reliable part of our Customer, providing him with high-quality and high-tech products on time.
STRATEGY
02
    We work in order to be the best in the DIY market (components for agricultural machinery, automotive parts and accessories) and electronics and meet all the needs of customers through the high quality of products supplied by our company and the provision of services. To do this, we:
  • through the constant efforts of the Company's employees, we ensure the competitive advantage of the products we supply;
  • we provide our customers with a wide assortment of products;
  • we provide high quality of the supplied products only from well-known and well-established manufacturers;
  • we organize the provision of related services to customers of our products.
  • we constantly optimize and control our costs, always keeps the quality of our services at the proper level, offering a product at the lowest market price.
ABOUT US
03
Ogter Group is a dynamically developing group of companies founded by Sergey Kirov in 2003. We constantly strive for excellence and change depending on market conditions. We work in the markets of DIY solutions supply (components for agricultural machinery, automotive parts and accessories) and electronics. Perhaps there is hardly a more dynamic industry in the economy and in business compared to the DIY market and the electronics market. We can not stop for a day, not for a minute, otherwise the market will go ahead, and we can fall behind irrevocably. The key to our success is the prosperity of our partners' business. We value our partners and it is thanks to our partners that we were able to achieve impressive business results, become the leading South European distributor of DIY and electronics solutions, both in the corporate supply market and in the direct sales segment.

For the Ogter group of companies one of the main types of business has traditionally been and is the sale of mineral fertilizers both in large and small wholesale and in retail. Over the years we have been building relationships with our customers – producers of agricultural products. Sensing the market and the needs of agricultural enterprises and farmers, having a direct channel of interaction with this category of our end consumers, we began to satisfy their needs for components for agricultural machinery, automotive parts, as well as an extensive list of products from the DIY category. As a result at the moment Ogter Group is a wide–profile distributor and supplier of DIY products (components for agricultural machinery, automotive parts and accessories) and consumer electronics and accessories, representing the interests of more than 20 well–known global manufacturers.

Our partners are 300 companies in all cities of Bulgaria and the most important points of Greece, Serbia and Romania. Our goal is to ensure maximum availability of our offers for each of our partners – be it a retail company, an online store, a system integrator, a regional distributor or a consumer cooperative.

We are not a retail company, we do not have shelves where we can show our assortment to the end customer, but we are a distribution “shelf” for more than 5,000 product items. Having a wide product range in stock, in 2017 we began the development of our sales through Internet channels. Representing the interests of more than 20 global manufacturers, Ogter Group performs one of the most important functions in the formation of the assortment of the Bulgarian national market both in retail and in the wholesale channel. Our specialists track the most important global trends, choose the best and most promising innovations of the DIY and electronics market in order to provide our company's customers with the widest possible range of modern products at competitive prices.

The wide range, stability, reliability, high reputation and professionalism of Ogter employees have earned the trust of not only 300 partner companies, but also our supplier partners. Ogter offers all its suppliers well-developed distribution channels, convenient logistics solutions, marketing support and product sales support throughout Bulgaria and at key points in Greece, Serbia and Romania.

Over these years our partners have developed in many directions, modernized their material and technical base - we supplied them with equipment and spare parts, they built office and industrial buildings, housing for employees – we helped them optimize costs and supplied them with construction and finishing materials, they upgraded their IT infrastructure – we supplied them with modern IT solutions. Ogter Group has always supported its partners, always went to meet their wishes and needs.
